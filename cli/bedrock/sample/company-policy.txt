Company Policy Document
=======================

1. Work Hours and Attendance
-----------------------------
- Standard work hours are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday.
- Core hours (required presence): 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM
- Flexible work arrangements may be available with manager approval.
- Remote work is permitted up to 3 days per week for eligible positions.
- All time off must be requested through the HR system at least 2 weeks in advance.

2. Leave Policy
---------------
Annual Leave:
- 0-2 years of service: 15 days per year
- 3-5 years of service: 20 days per year
- 6+ years of service: 25 days per year

Sick Leave:
- 10 days per year (with doctor's note for 3+ consecutive days)

Parental Leave:
- Primary caregiver: 16 weeks paid leave
- Secondary caregiver: 4 weeks paid leave

3. Expense Reimbursement
------------------------
Travel Expenses:
- Economy class for flights under 6 hours
- Business class available for flights over 6 hours (with approval)
- Hotel maximum: $200/night domestic, $300/night international
- Meal allowance: $75/day domestic, $100/day international

Equipment:
- Home office equipment up to $500 (one-time)
- Software subscriptions require IT approval

4. IT Security Policy
---------------------
- All devices must have full-disk encryption enabled
- Passwords must be at least 12 characters with complexity requirements
- Password changes required every 90 days
- Two-factor authentication is mandatory for all company systems
- Personal devices may not be used to access company data without MDM enrollment
- Report all security incidents to security@company.com within 24 hours

5. Code of Conduct
------------------
- Treat all colleagues with respect and professionalism
- Discrimination and harassment are strictly prohibited
- Maintain confidentiality of company and client information
- Conflicts of interest must be disclosed to management
- Social media posts about the company require PR approval

6. Performance Reviews
----------------------
- Annual performance reviews in December
- Mid-year check-ins in June
- Promotion cycles in January and July
- Bonus payouts in March (based on previous year performance)

7. Training and Development
---------------------------
- Annual training budget: $2,000 per employee
- Conference attendance: 1 approved conference per year
- Certification reimbursement available for job-related certifications
- Internal training programs offered quarterly

Contact HR at hr@company.com for any questions about company policies.
